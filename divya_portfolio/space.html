Space Management System




PROJECT DETAILS

Group: 3 members
Duration: 1 Month | 2012


Aim of the project

Object-oriented design is a method of design encompassing the process of object-oriented decomposition and a notation for depicting both logical and physical as well as static and dynamic models of the system under design. The main aim was to design a space management system for the college to manage halls, classrooms and parking spaces within the college premises.





Objective of Space Management System

Visitors enter the parking section and receive the token from the accountant who is present at the entrance. The parking slot which is free is assigned for the visitor and the details are updated by the parking system. Space management system allows the faculty to request the manager for a permanent parking slot. The manager assigns a parking slot for the faculty. It also allows the faculty or the student to book the hall or flex board. Events that are happening in the college are conducted in different halls based on the requirements. The student sends request for hall or flex to the manager. The manager allocates free halls/flex for the requested faculty. The student has to get a permission letter /confirmation letter from the faculty in-charge of the event for booking the hall. Once the permission is got the manager allocates hall/flex for the student/faculty.




Research and Analysis

Our research and analysis helped us gather information from faculty and students on various difficulties they face while booking halls, parking lot etc and came up with the design model that handles these.




UML DIAGRAMS

The Unified Modeling Language (UML) offers a way to visualize a system's architectural blueprints in a diagram (see image), including elements such as:
(1)Any activities (jobs)
(2)Individual components of the system
(3)And how they can interact with other software components.
(4)How the system will run
(5)How entities interact with others (components and interfaces)
(6)External user interface
The project aims at designing the UML design diagrams that are ready to use and can be a basis for understanding the whole system . It also supports higher-level development concepts such as collaborations, frameworks, patterns and components.
